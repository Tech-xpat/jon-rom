## 💰 Payment System

### Current Setup:
- CashApp ready (just add handle)
- Shows payment instructions
- Customer sends payment
- Admin confirms & ships

### Process:
1. Customer clicks cart
2. Sees total price
3. Sees CashApp handle
4. Opens CashApp app
5. Sends payment
6. Admin gets notification
7. Admin processes order

### To Configure:
1. Edit `ShopClient.tsx`
2. Find payment section
3. Replace "Coming Soon" with your handle
